# HUB Group (hub-group)

HUB is a global leader in multifunction pole design and street infrastructure, supplying over 1,000 pieces of urban infrastructure worldwide. With 20+ years of experience, HUB excels in integrating digital devices and data analytics into innovative urban solutions. Partnered with Valmont Industries, HUB delivers high-quality infrastructure for public spaces, including telecommunications poles, monopoles, steel jackets, and head frames.
